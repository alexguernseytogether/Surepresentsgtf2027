We're looking into it

Parking for 2027

Getting to and from the site is part of the day, especially if you're bringing the family or coming along as a group. For 2027 we're looking into whether to offer parking close to the festival.

We'd rather get this right than guess, so when you register you can tell us whether parking is something you'd actually use. The more people who say it would help, the more seriously we'll look at it.

To be clear, parking isn't part of the plan yet. If we do go ahead, it would be released separately with its own details and pricing.

A possible weekend option

Exploring a Motorhome Weekend Option

We're also exploring the possibility of a limited motorhome area for 2027.

The idea could give families and groups of friends the option to attend a possible Friday evening event, stay overnight on Friday, enjoy the festival on Saturday, stay overnight on Saturday and depart on Sunday morning.

The proposed motorhome area would be self-sufficient, with no water or electric connections available.

We're also considering whether to offer separate areas, such as a family motorhome zone and an adult-only motorhome zone. Children would be welcome in the family area, but they would need to stay with their responsible adult at all times. Motorhome bookings would need to be made by an adult aged 25 or over.

Tickets and motorhome spaces would be separate

A motorhome space wouldn't include festival entry ‚Äî everyone staying over would still need their own valid festival ticket.

And buying a festival ticket wouldn't include a motorhome space, parking or any overnight access.

If the motorhome area goes ahead, spaces would be limited and released separately, with their own terms, pricing and conditions.
